How to Play

How to Steal 101

Walk to the target to pick it up automatically. Then navigate back to the exit without being caught by a guard or entering a camera's cone of vision.

Difficulty

Guards move faster and see further. Hard adds one extra guard.

About

Each artwork carries centuries of survival, meaning, and belonging. Steal it in seconds. Then read what you've taken. Part of SLAM's Digital Learning Series on cultural heritage.
